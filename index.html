Welcome To Our Kathakalaya

Kathak is one of the eight classical dance forms of India, known for its graceful movements, intricate footwork, and expressive storytelling. Originating in the temples of North India, Kathak was a way to narrate stories of mythology and devotion through dance. Over time, it evolved into a blend of both traditional and Mughal influences, making it a unique representation of India’s rich heritage.

Kathak is more than just a dance; it’s an art form that combines rhythm (taal), music (sangeet), and expression (abhinaya), weaving together stories that resonate with the human experience.
